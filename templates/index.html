Machine and Deep Learning for Stress Detection

This project aims to develop and compare machine learning (Random Forest, Decision Tree, Logistic Regression) and deep learning (MLP) models for accurate stress detection using multimodal physiological data. The goal is to evaluate their performance in classifying stress states and identify the most effective approach for real-time mental health monitoring applications..
