About Our Stress Detection Platform

Why Stress Detection?

Stress detection using physiological data is critical in advancing mental healthcare and improving well-being. It enables continuous, non-invasive monitoring of mental states in real time.

ML & DL Models Used

We explore various models including Multilayer Perceptron (MLP), Random Forest, Decision Tree, and Logistic Regression. These models help in classifying stress and non-stress states from multimodal physiological signals.

Feature Engineering & Accuracy

The project leverages feature extraction and preprocessing to enhance prediction accuracy. Tree-based models like Random Forest and Decision Tree demonstrate reliable performance, while MLP captures complex stress patterns effectively.

Real-Time Mental Health Monitoring

This research supports the development of real-time, automated stress detection systems. The findings highlight the significance of model selection and optimization in building reliable tools for mental health analytics and proactive intervention.
